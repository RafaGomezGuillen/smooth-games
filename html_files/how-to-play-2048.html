The goal of this game is simple, join the tiles and get to 2048.

Buttons

Use your arrow or WASD keys to move the tiles. If you want to undo press Z or the button Undo

Titles

 If you want to undo press Z or the button Undo

SCORE

Score tells you the actual points you have.

BEST

BEST tells you the higher points that you have ever scored.
